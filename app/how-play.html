How to play?

quiZone is a location based quizzes where player can interact with environment around through smartphone

Step 1

Screen with multiple images invites you to play quiz.

It shows all answers of current quiz.

Step 2

Enter Game Pin at quizone.me or quiZone App.

Game Pin is a 4 digits on a screen (1234*).

Step 3

Game started. Check your question and find an answer.

To answer press the number corresponding to the number next to each image on the screen
