Frequently Asked Questions

What is quizone?

Quizone is a platform to enjoy playing quizzes. Players can play together. Actions of one player helps others to find their own answers. It is very different from multi choice quizzes. Quizone creates a friendly environment among players and stimulates interactivity between players.

How it works?

In quizone, multiple images on a screen are shown. This screen is visible to all players. Players get different questions on each player's smartphone.

How is quizone different from usual quizzes?

Quizone concept is very different from the standard multiple choice quizzes, and allows players to enjoy answering their questions without been distracted by faster players, and in the same time they learn from each player actions.

It is a matching game between the question and the answer, multiple players collecting a puzzle together.

Why do I need quizone?

People naturally like intrigue, collecting and puzzles. Quizone is an easy to create environment to get people together, ice break an event, promote a topic or just educate people in a friendly environment.

What is special about quizone?

The problem with standard public quizzes, is that there are always some people faster than others in answers, some people have to wait others, or do not get an opportunity to seek an answer as someone else answered it before them.

In Quizone, the questions are distributed among all players so that everyone can take as much time as they need for their question. People who answer fast, do not need to wait, as they move into next questions. The actions of players are visible to others, helping each other to find the right answers.

How to play quizone?

Anyone can join quizone at the spot. Players only need their personal smartphone, open quizone.me web site or quiZone App and join the game.

What is quizone Spot?

Quizone spot is a place where quizone can be played. It is basically a screen visible by players to join and play.

Who is quizone Host?

The quizone Host is a registered person who has the quizone software package and has the privilege to run quizone engine on his/her computer so people can play.

What is quizone Engine?

Quizone Engine is a software that runs on the host computer and projects the quizone on the screen visible by the players. The host can control his engine via quizone Studio.

What is quizone Studio?

A host runs quizone Engine on a screen for the players. The host uses a quizone Studio, which is accessible to the host at the quizone web site. In Studio the host can start the game, choose the quizones to play, end the game and see statistics.

Is quizone free?

Yes. Quizone is Pre-release stage, anyone who wants to try quizone can apply a Request form to become a quizone host at quizone.me/about

The quizone administration keeps its right to accept or reject any application.

There are no charge for hosts to use quizone; hosts are encouraged to feedback on their experience and send video and image materials of their experience to quizone team.

Quizone administration keeps its right to revoke the host rights of its users without notice.

What do I need to become a quizone Host?

If you want to use quizone platform, you need to become a Host. For that you need to fill the Request form at quizone.me/about. The administrator will check your application, approve or decline and send you a link to download quizone Engine for your spot.

Can quizone be played online?

Absolutely. Using zoom, the host can share the screen of the quizone Engine, and all zoom attendee can play quizone using their computer or smartphone via the web site or App.

Can I make my own quizone games?

Quizone is optimized for that. Host can use the quizone builder tool provided with quizone Engine to create own quizones and upload them to the cloud to play. Public quizones can be shared among all hosts, while your private quizones are only for you.

What data is collected by quizone?

Quizone does not collect any user data for any commercial purposes, and never shares the data with any other party. The user and host data is used for improving the user experience. For details refer to the terms of privacy at https://quizone.me/privacy
