What is quiZone?

quiZone is an interactive quiz platform where participants interact with the objects in question using their mobile devices.

Why quiZone?

A different way to provide answers to the public brings collaboration between players and the environment as well as between players themselves.

Where quiZone?

As a location-based game, quiZone perfectly suitable, but not limited, for museums, galleries, specials events, education, home parties and etc.

How does quiZone works?

With quiZone multiple images are shown on a screen and visible to all players.

Players need only their mobile devices and Internet connection. Using web-browser or quiZone App each players received a unique question and looking for answer on a screen. To answer player tap image number or make a snap-shot*.

* App user only

quiZone is a great choice nowadays

Lets see why quiZone is good solution during the pandemic in a two simple examples.

Example 1. Entertainment

Museums, galleries, events.

  • Alive exhibits with quizzes.
  • No more touchscreens stations and extra devices involved.
  • Players use their smartphones connected to Internet.

Example 2. Education

Classrooms, conferences.

  • Combine slides and quizzes in one lesson.
  • Offer your students interactive experience in class or via Zoom.
  • Boost collaboration and teamwork during lesson.
  • Fun way to wrap lesson up.
